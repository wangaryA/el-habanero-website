Our Story

A Taste of Mexico's Coastline

Nestled in the heart of Burien, El Habanero Bar & Grill brings the vibrant flavors of Mexican coastal cuisine to the Pacific Northwest.

Our kitchen celebrates the rich traditions of Mexican seafood, from perfectly prepared aguachiles and ceviches to our signature grilled octopus. Every dish tells a story of passion, authenticity, and the bold spirit of the habanero pepper.
